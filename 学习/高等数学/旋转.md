# 旋转
绕x轴旋转
![[曲面方程 2024-01-15 07.39.23.excalidraw]]

$$
\begin{array} \\
YOZ面内，设曲线f(y,z)=0 \\
z=z_{1},d=\sqrt{ x^{2}+y^{2} }=|y_{1}| \implies y_{1}= \pm \sqrt{ x^{2}+y^{2} }\\
f(\pm \sqrt{ x^{2}+y^{2} },z)=0
\end{array}
$$

同理绕y轴
$$
\begin{array} \\
YOZ面内，曲线f(y,z)=0 \\
旋转后f(y,\pm \sqrt{ x^{2}+z^{2} })
\end{array}
$$
